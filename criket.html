Mahendra Singh Dhoni(MS Dhoni) was the captain of India when the team won the inaugural T20 World Cup in 2007.

Younis Khan was the captain of Pakistan's 2009 T20 World Cup winning team.

Paul Collingwood was the captain of England's 2010 T20 World Cup winning team.

Darren Sammy was the captain of the West Indies cricket team in the 2012 ICC World Twenty20.the first major trophy for the West Indies in eight years.

Lasith Malinga was the captain of Sri Lanka's 2014 T20 World Cup winning team.

Darren Sammy was the captain of the West Indies cricket team when they won the 2016 T20 World Cup. Carlos Brathwaite, a key member of the team, hit the winning six in the final against England.

Aaron Finch was the captain of the Australian cricket team during the 2021 ICC T20 World Cup.claiming their first T20 World Cup title.

In 2022, Buttler became the second England captain to win the ICC Men's T20 World Cup.
